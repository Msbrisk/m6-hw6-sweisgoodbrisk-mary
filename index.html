About Me

I have worked in the wine and spirits industry for almost 25 years and am currently a wine consultant for the second largest distributor in North America. I am also a student at the University of Florida pursuing a Masters degree in Political Communication. I am looking forward to graduating this summer and pursuing a new career in political communication.
